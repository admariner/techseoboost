 The festival was founded in 2009 by the late Michael Jackson and his wife Janet Jackson. 
 
 It has been held at various locations around the world including Las Vegas, New York, Miami, Los Angeles, London, Paris, Rome, Amsterdam, Berlin, Hamburg, Mexico City, Sydney, Melbourne, Perth, Cape Town, Tokyo, Osaka, Shanghai, Hong Kong, Singapore, Manila, Bangkok, Jakarta, Kuala Lumpur, Seoul, Beijing, Shanghai and Sydney.

 In 2014, it attracted more than two million people from all over the world. 
 
 This year, organisers said they were expecting about one million visitors - but that figure could be affected if weather conditions are poor. 
 
 They have also warned of "extreme weather", with temperatures expected to drop below freezing on Saturday night.
