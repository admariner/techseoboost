 The artist is known as 'RiRi ' and she has been featured in many magazines including Vogue, Glamour, Vanity Fair & Elle Magazine. 
 
 She was born in South Africa but moved to London at age 7 where her father worked as an artist. 
 
 Her mother died when she was 12 so she grew up alone until moving back home after graduating from college. 
 
 In 2008 she started taking photographs professionally and now lives in Los Angeles. 
 
 A few months ago she released a book called 'The Art Of Rihanna' which features some stunning images taken by her. 
 
 It also includes exclusive behind-the-scenes photos
