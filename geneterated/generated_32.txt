Links Selena Gomez says "My lil brother just released his second song!! Check it out: " 
 
 The song is called "I Don't Wanna Live Without Your Love" and it's a duet with the late rapper Tupac Shakur. 
 
 In an interview on "The Breakfast Club" Thursday, Gomez, 19, said she was inspired to write the song. 
 
 "It's about my little brother," she said. "He wrote it. He did all of the vocals. I didn't even know he had done it." 
 
 Gomez's older brother, Rocco, died in September at age 27. 
 
 She also revealed that her younger sister, Dream, has been diagnosed with cancer. 
 
 Dream appeared as one of the stars of the hit Disney Channel show "Wizards Of Waverly Place."

 Gomez added that she's "super excited" for fans who have supported her. 
 
 "I'm so grateful for everyone who's reached out to me through social media or sent me messages via email telling me how much they love me," she wrote. 
 
 Her new single will be available digitally Oct. 29.
