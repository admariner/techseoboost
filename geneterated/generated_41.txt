 Rice was the U.S. ambassador to the United Nations from 2009 until 2013. 
 
 She has been an outspoken critic of President Donald Trump since he took office in January. 
 
 On Sunday, she said that Trump's "divisive, petty, personal attacks" against his predecessor were not helpful to America's standing abroad. 
 
 "I think it's important we have a president who can speak with one voice when it comes to foreign policy," Rice said. 
 
 But she also criticized him over North Korea, saying its nuclear program poses "a grave threat to all Americans." 
 
 "The world must never accept this level of threat," she said.​
